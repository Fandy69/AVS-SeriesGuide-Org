Unlock all features

Get the app, then:

  1. At the bottom, tap More .
  2. Tap Unlock all features.

Choose one of the subscription options. Every subscription unlocks all features.

After a 30 day free trial the subscription is paid yearly. Cancel it any time in the Play Store or Amazon app.

Restore X Pass app purchase

If you have purchased the X Pass app in the past, find X Pass on Google Play and install it. While installed, the X Pass app will unlock all features in SeriesGuide.

What do you get, why should you pay?

Regular Updates and Features

SeriesGuide is developed as and built upon open source software and all but some convenience features are available for free. If you really like it, paying for it will help me make future features and fixes.

However, you can also help by sending in feedback, translating on crowdin or even forking the code and adding features. SeriesGuide would not exist without its community!
